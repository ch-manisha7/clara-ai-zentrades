[Sales Rep]: Hi, glad you could make it. Tell me about your company.

[Sales Rep]: And how's your call volume?

[Client]: Moderate. Maybe 20-30 calls a day. But the ones that matter most are the emergencies. A sprinkler head discharging in a commercial building is a big deal — water damage, business interruption, potential liability.

[Sales Rep]: What defines an emergency for Cascade?

[Client]: We have a phone tree. Office number first, then our service manager's cell, then our backup tech. We haven't formalized the timeout between attempts.

[Client]: Our office is at 3300 Airport Way South, Seattle, Washington 98134.
